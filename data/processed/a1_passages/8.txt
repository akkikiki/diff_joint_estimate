David Brown

David Brown is a basketball player from New York, and he is ready to win! "I don't only play basketball, " he says, " I do a lot of other things to stay healthy. I run, ride my bicycle, and play soccer, too. It's also really important to eat healthy food". David is now famous in his hometown, and people all over the country know his name. "I don't love being famous, but I do love to play ball!", David says. "The hardest part is people always take a lot of pictures of me, and sometimes I see my personal information in magazines. People also take pictures of my wife, she hates it." Being famous is hard, but David has no plans to stop playing. "I just want to win for my team and my fans," he says, "and then I can be happy.""
