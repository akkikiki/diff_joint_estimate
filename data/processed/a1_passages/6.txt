New movies

The King
The new movie 'The King' is not very good. I am not sure if the people are real actors! The movie was really boring, and I left after the first fifteen minutes.

Out of State
This movie was a lot of fun. Two good friends go to college together in a different state. They do not study very much, but they go to a lot of parties! One of the friends meets a girl at a dance, and they of course fall in love. The movie was very cute.

Going Home
This drama was very sad sometimes, and was difficult to watch. An old dog travels across the country to find its owners. The dog is welcomed by many people, but all the dog wants is to find its family. A wonderful movie, but sad!
