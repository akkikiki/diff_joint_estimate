Art festival

What is your hobby? Do you love to draw and paint pictures? Are you interested in art? Then come to the art festival! In February there is a big festival in our city, and there are many fun activities. In the morning there is a music class, and at lunch famous chefs cook food for everyone. There are art classes for kids, and you can also buy art to take home. The festival is the best event of the whole year! See you and your family there!
